Howdy! I’m Chau Tran, but you can call me Kacy. I’m currently double majoring in Computer Science and Data Science, and yes, I officially declared them because I like committing to my chaos early. I’ve always been curious about finance and the world of big data, which explains why I’m drawn to becoming an AI engineer or maybe even a quant researcher someday. I enjoy working on projects where I can build models, experiment with machine learning, and turn messy data into something that actually makes sense.

Now you might be wondering, what about retiring? Honestly, I do not really picture myself retiring quietly. I want to start my own company one day and build something meaningful from scratch. But my mom gave me some very practical advice. She told me to learn about legal matters first before doing anything big, mostly to avoid legal trouble and, as she dramatically puts it, to stay out of jail. So for now, I am dreaming big, coding hard, learning about business and maybe even law on the side, and slowly preparing myself to build something of my own in the future.
